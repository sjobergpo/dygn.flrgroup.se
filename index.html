DYGN App

This app is designed to help anyone who, for various reasons, has difficulty perceiving the time of day—or simply wants a widget on their Apple Watch. Use DYGN for iOS to view and adjust the time intervals used on your Apple Watch, and DYGN for watchOS to access the widget and complication.

For support, email: dygnapp@gmail.com
